Our History

TechCon was founded in 2018 with a vision to create a space where tech professionals and enthusiasts could come together to share knowledge and spark innovation. Starting as a small gathering of local developers, it has grown into an international event attracting thousands of attendees and featuring some of the biggest names in the industry.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower the next generation of technologists by providing a platform for learning, collaboration, and professional growth. We are committed to fostering an inclusive environment where everyone, regardless of background, can contribute to and benefit from the advancement of technology. Our goal is to inspire and equip our community to solve the world's most pressing challenges.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Evelyn Reed

Dr. Evelyn Reed is a leading data scientist renowned for her work in ethical AI development. Her keynote at TechCon 2022 on "Bias in Algorithms" was a landmark talk that influenced industry standards and sparked important conversations about responsible technology.

Marcus Chen

Marcus Chen, a cybersecurity expert, delivered a powerful presentation at TechCon 2023 on the state of network security. His insights into protecting digital infrastructure from emerging threats provided attendees with crucial, actionable knowledge.
